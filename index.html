About Riverstone Capital Advisors

Riverstone Capital is a boutique investment bank providing services in capital raising, M&A and strategic FP&A services to clients. We specialize in early to growth stage companies. Led by highly experienced ex-Credit Suisse investment bankers, we have significant experience across sectors including New Age Technology, Retail & Consumer, Healthcare, Enterprise Tech and Financial Services.

Our Services

  • Equity and Debt Fund Raising
  • Strategic Advisory

Fund Raising

We partner and run end-to-end capital raise processes for clients. We believe that capital raise is not just a financial milestone, however, an important strategic milestone for a company unlocking next frontier of growth. We partner with our clients in this strategic initiative with a 360 degree approach of advisory ensuring best outcomes.

Strategic Advisory

We provide solutions and advisory to companies and stakeholders in their sell side or divestment initiatives. We also run end-to-end transaction process ensuring the best outcomes for the stakeholders upon M&A, divestments, financial restructurings and other strategic matters.
